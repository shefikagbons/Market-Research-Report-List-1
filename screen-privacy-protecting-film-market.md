<p><p>The Screen Privacy Protecting Film market is primarily divided into two types: Tempered Glass Film and TPU Film. Tempered Glass Film offers robust protection against impacts and scratches while maintaining high clarity and touch sensitivity. In contrast, TPU Film is more flexible and offers a thinner profile, providing basic protection against scratches and minor impacts while preserving the device's design. Both types enhance user privacy by limiting screen visibility from side angles, catering to varying consumer preferences and device protection needs.</p></p>
