<p><strong>The Aircraft Leasing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Leasing</li><li>Wet Leasing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft leasing market consists primarily of two types: dry leasing and wet leasing. Dry leasing involves leasing an aircraft without crew, maintenance, or insurance, primarily used by airlines that already operate these functions. In contrast, wet leasing includes the provision of the aircraft along with crew, maintenance, and insurance, typically utilized for short-term needs or to quickly expand capacity. Both leasing types serve different operational requirements, offering flexibility and financial efficiency to airline operators.</p></p>
