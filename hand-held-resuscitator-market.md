<p><strong>The Hand-held Resuscitator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-inflating Resuscitator</li><li>Flow-inflating Resuscitator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hand-held resuscitator market is primarily divided into two types: self-inflating resuscitators and flow-inflating resuscitators. Self-inflating resuscitators automatically refill with air after compression, making them portable and easy to use in emergencies. In contrast, flow-inflating resuscitators require a gas source to inflate the bag and provide a continuous flow of oxygen, offering more controlled ventilation. Both types are essential in medical environments for effective patient resuscitation, catering to different clinical needs and scenarios.</p></p>
