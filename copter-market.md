<p><p>The copter market is categorized into three main types: Single-rotor, Coaxial Twin Rotor, and Split Shaft Twin Rotor. Single-rotor copters are known for their high efficiency and maneuverability, making them suitable for various applications. Coaxial Twin Rotors feature two rotors stacked vertically, enhancing stability and lift without increasing the aircraft's footprint. Split Shaft Twin Rotor systems utilize two shafts to drive separate rotors, offering improved redundancy and power distribution, which enhances performance and reliability in diverse operational conditions.</p></p>
