<p><strong>The Automotive Seat Belt Pretensioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>anchor and buckle pretensioners</li><li>seat belt retractor pretensioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive seat belt pretensioner market comprises three primary types: anchor pretensioners, buckle pretensioners, and seat belt retractor pretensioners. Anchor pretensioners are mounted at the seat belt anchor point, quickly tightening the belt in a collision. Buckle pretensioners are integrated into the buckle itself, retracting the belt when a crash is detected. Seat belt retractor pretensioners are built into the retractor mechanism, enhancing belt tightness and ensuring occupants remain securely restrained during sudden stops or accidents, improving overall safety.</p></p>
