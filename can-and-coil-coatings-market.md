<p><p>The can and coil coatings market is segmented into three main types: solventborne, waterborne, and others. Solventborne coatings utilize organic solvents for application, offering excellent performance but raising environmental concerns due to volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Waterborne coatings, on the other hand, use water as the primary solvent, making them more environmentally friendly while still providing good durability and adhesion. The "others" category may include powder coatings and high-solids coatings, which offer unique properties suited for specific applications in the packaging and construction industries.</p></p>
