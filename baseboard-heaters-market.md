<p><p>Baseboard heaters are essential heating systems available in two primary market types: electric and hydronic. Electric baseboard heaters operate using electric power to generate heat, providing quick and efficient warmth in individual rooms. Hydronic baseboard heaters, on the other hand, circulate hot water from a boiler through pipes, offering uniform warmth and enhanced energy efficiency. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with electric models suited for quick heating and hydronic models preferred for larger spaces and lower operational costs.</p></p>
