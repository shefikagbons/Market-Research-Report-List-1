<p><p>The centrifugal chemical pump market encompasses three main types: hydraulic, mechanical, and solenoid pumps. Hydraulic pumps utilize fluid power to facilitate the movement of chemicals and are ideal for high-capacity applications. Mechanical pumps rely on rotational energy to create flow, offering efficiency and versatility in various chemical processes. Solenoid pumps employ electromagnetic forces to achieve precise flow control, making them suitable for applications requiring accuracy in low-volume transfers. Together, these types cater to diverse industrial needs in handling chemicals.</p></p>
