<p><p>The gas meter market comprises two primary types: ordinary gas meters and smart gas meters. Ordinary gas meters measure gas consumption using mechanical mechanisms, providing basic, real-time data to utility companies. In contrast, smart gas meters integrate advanced technology, enabling two-way communication between the meter and the utility. This provides real-time data, remote monitoring, and enhanced accuracy in billing. The shift towards smart meters is driven by the demand for efficiency, improved energy management, and consumer engagement in energy usage.</p></p>
