<p><ul><li>Point FOS</li><li>Distributed FOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High-Speed Fiber Optic Sensor market is categorized into two main types: Point Fiber Optic Sensors (FOS) and Distributed Fiber Optic Sensors (FOS). Point FOS are designed to measure specific parameters, such as temperature or strain, at discrete locations along the fiber, making them ideal for localized monitoring. In contrast, Distributed FOS provide continuous data along the entire length of the fiber, enabling real-time monitoring over large areas, thus offering comprehensive insights into environmental or structural changes.</p></p>
