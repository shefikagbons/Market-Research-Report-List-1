<p><strong>The Nanosecond Pulsed Fiber Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Q-Switched Fiber Laser</li><li>Mode-Locked Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nanosecond pulsed fiber laser market primarily includes two types: Q-Switched Fiber Lasers and Mode-Locked Fiber Lasers. Q-Switched Fiber Lasers generate high-energy pulses by creating a delay in the laser cavity, ideal for material processing and marking applications. In contrast, Mode-Locked Fiber Lasers produce a train of ultra-short pulses, enabling high peak power and precision suitable for nonlinear imaging and spectroscopy. Both technologies cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, telecommunications, and medical applications, driving market growth and innovation.</p></p>
