<p><p>The heat-curable protective coating materials market is primarily segmented into one-component and two-component systems. One-component coatings are ready-to-use formulations that cure upon exposure to heat, simplifying application and reducing the risk of mixing errors. In contrast, two-component coatings require the combination of a curing agent and resin before application, allowing for enhanced performance and durability. Both types are used to protect surfaces from corrosion, chemicals, and wear, making them essential in various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
