<p><strong>The Gravel Paver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tracked Pavers</li><li>Wheeled Pavers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gravel paver market is primarily divided into tracked pavers and wheeled pavers. Tracked pavers utilize continuous tracks for enhanced traction and stability on uneven terrain, making them ideal for large-scale construction and infrastructure projects. They offer better weight distribution and are suited for soft or slippery surfaces. Conversely, wheeled pavers are more maneuverable and faster on solid ground, making them suitable for smaller jobs and urban environments. Both types cater to different operational needs in the gravel paving industry.</p></p>
