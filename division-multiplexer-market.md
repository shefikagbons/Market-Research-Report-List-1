<p><strong>The Division Multiplexer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FDM</li><li>WDM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Division Multiplexer market primarily consists of Frequency Division Multiplexing (FDM) and Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM). FDM separates different signals by allocating distinct frequency bands to each, enabling simultaneous transmission over a single medium, often used in radio and TV broadcasting. In contrast, WDM focuses on fiber-optic networks, utilizing different wavelengths of light to transmit multiple signals over a single fiber, significantly increasing bandwidth capacity. Both technologies enhance communication efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
