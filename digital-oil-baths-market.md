<p><ul><li>Circulating</li><li>Non-Circulating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Oil Baths market is classified into two primary types: circulating and non-circulating. Circulating digital oil baths continuously circulate oil through a system, providing effective heat transfer and temperature uniformity, making them ideal for applications requiring stable conditions. In contrast, non-circulating digital oil baths maintain temperature without fluid movement, often used for smaller scale or specialized processes where consistent heat is vital but fluid circulation is unnecessary. Each type serves distinct industrial and laboratory needs.</p></p>
