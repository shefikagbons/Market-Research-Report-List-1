<p><p>The Automotive Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Systems market includes two primary types: Pneumatic EGR Valves and Electric EGR Valves. Pneumatic EGR Valves use vacuum pressure to control the flow of recirculated exhaust gases, providing a cost-effective solution for managing emissions. In contrast, Electric EGR Valves utilize electronic controls for precise regulation, offering improved response times and efficiency. Both types contribute to reducing nitrogen oxide emissions, enhancing fuel efficiency, and meeting stringent environmental regulations in modern vehicles.</p></p>
