<p><p>Home non-invasive ventilators for Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) are classified into two main types: single-level ventilators and bilevel ventilators. Single-level ventilators deliver a constant air pressure, providing steady airflow to help maintain airway patency. In contrast, bilevel ventilators offer two distinct pressure levels—one for inhalation and a lower one for exhalation—facilitating easier breathing and enhanced comfort. Both types aim to improve respiratory function and quality of life for COPD patients in a home setting.</p></p>
