<p><strong>The Defibrillation Pacing Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biphasic Waveform</li><li>Single-phase Waveform</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The defibrillation pacing monitor market is primarily categorized into biphasic and single-phase waveform types. Biphasic waveform devices deliver shocks in two phases, increasing efficacy and reducing myocardial injury, making them the preferred choice in modern resuscitation. Single-phase waveform devices, which deliver shocks in one continuous direction, are less commonly used today but may still find application in specific scenarios. Both types are crucial for managing cardiac arrhythmias and improving patient outcomes in emergency medical situations.</p></p>
