<p><p>The Ship Echo Sounder market is primarily categorized into two types: Dual Frequency Echo Sounders and Single Frequency Echo Sounders. Dual Frequency Echo Sounders utilize two different frequencies, enhancing depth measurement accuracy and enabling users to differentiate between various seabed types and underwater structures. In contrast, Single Frequency Echo Sounders operate at one frequency, making them simpler and cost-effective for basic applications. Both types serve critical roles in marine navigation, fishing, and underwater surveying, catering to varying operational needs.</p></p>
