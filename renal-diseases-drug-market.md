<p><p>The renal diseases drug market is divided into two primary segments: Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) drugs and End-Stage Kidney Disease (ESRD) drugs. CKD drugs focus on managing the progression of kidney impairment, addressing complications like hypertension and anemia, while promoting kidney health. In contrast, ESRD drugs are tailored for patients undergoing dialysis or preparing for kidney transplantation, aiming to manage symptoms and improve survival rates. Both segments cater to a growing patient population, driven by increasing incidences of renal disorders.</p></p>
