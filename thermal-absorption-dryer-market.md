<p><p>The thermal absorption dryer market is segmented into two main types: intermittent and continuous dryers. Intermittent dryers operate in batch processes, absorbing moisture during specific cycles, making them suitable for smaller, fluctuating production needs. Continuous dryers, on the other hand, provide a steady stream of drying, operating non-stop to handle large volumes of material efficiently. Both types offer distinct advantages in terms of energy efficiency and operational flexibility, catering to varying industrial requirements and production capacities.</p></p>
