The market includes Through-Silicon Via (TSV), which utilizes vertical interconnects for high-density integration; Silicon Interposer, which offers a substrate for multiple chips to communicate efficiently; and Through-Glass Via (TGV), enabling connections through glass substrates for enhanced insulation and lower parasitics. Other market segments may include advanced packaging and hybrid integration technologies, catering to varied application needs in electronics.</p></p>
