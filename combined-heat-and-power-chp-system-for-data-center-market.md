<p><p>Combined Heat and Power (CHP) systems, also known as cogeneration systems, are efficient energy systems that simultaneously generate electricity and useful heat from the same energy source. In data centers, which demand significant energy for both computing and cooling, CHP systems offer a dual advantage by improving energy efficiency and reducing operational costs. The market for CHP systems in data centers is witnessing notable growth driven by the increasing need for energy efficiency, rising electricity costs, and growing awareness of sustainable practices.</p><p>The Combined Heat and Power (CHP) System for Data Center Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.4% during the forecast period. As data centers increasingly prioritize sustainability goals, many are adopting CHP systems to lower their carbon footprint while ensuring reliable power supply.
