<p><p>Crawl space encapsulation services involve sealing and protecting crawl spaces to enhance energy efficiency and prevent moisture intrusion. The market is primarily divided into two types: plastic-based and concrete-based encapsulation. Plastic-based encapsulation employs durable polyethylene barriers to create a moisture-resistant seal, while concrete-based methods involve pouring concrete to form a solid, impermeable barrier. Both types aim to improve indoor air quality, reduce pest infestations, and enhance property value, but they cater to different structural and budgetary needs.</p></p>
