<p><p>The Tobacco Films market features various types based on shrinkage properties: </p><p>1. **Non-Shrink Films** remain stable under heat, ensuring consistent dimensions, often used for high-visibility packaging.</p><p>2. **Medium Shrink Films** offer a balance between flexibility and coverage, providing moderate shrinkage ideal for packaging diverse tobacco products.</p><p>3. **High-Shrink Films** contract significantly when heated, allowing for tailored and tightly-fitted packaging, enhancing product appeal and protection.</p><p>4. **Printable Shrink Films** are designed for high-quality graphics and branding, facilitating effective marketing while maintaining shrink characteristics for aesthetic packaging.</p></p>
