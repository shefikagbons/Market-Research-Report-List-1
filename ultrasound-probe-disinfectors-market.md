<p><p>The ultrasound probe disinfectors market can be divided into two main types based on the level of disinfection they provide. Intermediate/Low-Level Disinfection (LLDs/ILDs) focuses on eliminating a broad spectrum of pathogens, suitable for semi-critical instruments, while High-Level Disinfection (HLD) offers a more extensive sterilization process, effectively killing all microorganisms except large numbers of bacterial spores, making it ideal for critical instruments. Each type addresses specific infection control needs in healthcare settings for ultrasound probes.</p></p>
