Igneous phosphate deposits are formed from magmatic processes and are often rich in rare minerals. Other types include sedimentary and metamorphic deposits, which vary in quality and accessibility. Each type has unique extraction processes and applications, influencing market dynamics and supply chains in the agricultural and industrial sectors.</p></p>
