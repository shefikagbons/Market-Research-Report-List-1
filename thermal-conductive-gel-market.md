<p><p>The thermal conductive gel market is mainly categorized into two types: one-component and two-component thermal conductive gels. One-component gels are pre-mixed and ready to use, offering convenience and consistency in applications, often suitable for consumer electronics and smaller devices. In contrast, two-component gels require a mixing process before application, providing customized thermal conductivity and curing properties, ideal for industrial applications and high-performance electronics. Both types are essential for effective heat management in various technological sectors.</p></p>
