<p><strong>The Electronic Ignition Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single coil</li><li>Multi-coil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic ignition module market is categorized into single-coil and multi-coil systems. Single-coil systems utilize one ignition coil to fire multiple cylinders, offering simplicity and lower costs, making them suitable for older or budget-friendly vehicles. In contrast, multi-coil systems use individual coils for each cylinder, providing enhanced efficiency, better performance, and improved fuel economy. These are commonly found in modern vehicles, catering to the demand for advanced ignition systems that optimize engine performance and reduce emissions.</p></p>
