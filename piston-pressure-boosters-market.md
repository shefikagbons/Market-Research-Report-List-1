<p><strong>The Piston Pressure Boosters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Double Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piston pressure boosters are devices used to increase gas or liquid pressure and come in two main types: single-stage and double-stage. Single-stage boosters elevate pressure in one compression cycle, making them ideal for applications requiring moderate pressure increases. In contrast, double-stage boosters compress in two stages, achieving higher pressures efficiently, suitable for demanding applications. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, balancing performance, energy efficiency, and application requirements. Their selection depends on the specific pressure and volume demands of the process.</p></p>
